## Role

The gamification audit deeply analyzes the game mechanics integrated into the application. It evaluates engagement loops, progression systems, rewards, user retention, onboarding, economic balance, monetization integration, UX quality and compares them to industry standards.

## Use Cases

- **Engagement loops**: analyze the frequency and quality of user interactions
- **Progression systems**: evaluate the clarity and motivation of milestones
- **Rewards**: measure the impact of incentives on retention
- **Onboarding**: verify that the first steps in the application are engaging
- **Benchmark**: compare gamification scores to industry standards

## Triggers

The planner agent loads this skill when:
- A gamification audit request is made
- The user wants to check their application's engagement and retention

## Inputs

- Application codebase (frontend and backend)
- User metrics if available (retention, sessions, conversion)
- Product documentation (PROPOSITION_VALEUR.md, specs)

## Outputs

- Structured audit report with scores per dimension
- Prioritized recommendations to improve each engagement loop
- Benchmark comparison with industry standards
